About Me

I'm a postdoctoral researcher at the Centre for the Advanced Study of Collective Behaviour. My current research seeks to develop modeling metholodies to investigate collective behavior emerging from natural and artificial systems. I hold a PhD in Theoretical Computer Science from Inria & ENS-PSL in Paris, supervised by Jérôme Feret. My PhD subject sought to verify assumptions in logical models, a popular Systems Biology modeling tool. You can read more about this work here.

Research interests:

  • Abstract interpretation
  • Static analysis of biomedical devices (such as digital twins)
  • Modeling paradigms for Systems Biology
  • Graph rewriting systems (in particular, KappaLanguage)
